Bio Energy

Our Story

Engineering biomethane
for India's clean energy future.

Our objectives of the energy turnaround can only be achieved in an economically justifiable way if biomethane is used as an immediately available option to lower the carbon freight of the energy sector — in a future energy system that is increasingly fed by wind.

With successful projects in Maharashtra, Kerala, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh, Uzzala continues to expand its footprint across India.

Our Vision

We are committed to ensure that our comprehensive business solutions spearhead the renewable energy movement for a cleaner and greener environment globally, by aggressively capitalising the emerging Grid and Off Grid opportunities, biogas turnkey projects, biogas to biomethane (CNG) and biogas to electricity in appropriate collaboration with the world's leading renewable energy companies.

Our Mission

Our mission to strive hard to achieve what has not been achieved hitherto and produce the world's best products & services in terms of quality, reliability and performance to serve the domain of biogas and solar energy, and translate our advanced technologies into value for our customers and stakeholders.
