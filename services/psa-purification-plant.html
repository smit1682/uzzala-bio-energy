PSA Purification Plant

Purification

PSA Purification Plant

Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) uses specially engineered carbon molecular sieves or zeolites to selectively adsorb CO₂, moisture, nitrogen, and oxygen from biogas under pressure, then release them during depressurization. This produces high-purity biomethane suitable for grid injection.

Our PSA systems feature multiple adsorption vessels operating in a coordinated cycle to ensure continuous gas output. The modular design allows capacity expansion without shutting down the plant.

Key Features

High Purity

Achieves 96–99% methane concentration through multi-vessel adsorption cycling.

Modular Design

Expandable vessel configuration allows capacity growth without plant shutdown.

No Liquid Waste

Purely gas-phase process — no wastewater or chemical sludge generated.
